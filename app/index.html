Today’s university requires technology that enables connections anywhere on campus at any time. See the journey and consider how your enterprise resembles a distributed campus environment.

Meet Tyler. Tyler often pulls late nights studying, making it tough for him to get up for his 8 a.m. class. After hitting snooze several times, he looks to see it is 8:10.

Tyler quickly checks into class on his iPhone and streams the lecture onto his TV as the professor kicks off a new section. Tyler quickly gets dressed and heads out the door, through the dorm while still connected to the lecture on his phone.

Students today expect seamless communication between multiple devices to manage their busy schedules.
